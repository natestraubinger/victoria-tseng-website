About Victoria

A World of Experience,
Rooted in the Bay Area

Victoria is a long-time resident of the East Bay and San Francisco. A graduate of UC Berkeley and Cornell Law School, she initially practiced law at a private firm before serving as in-house counsel for Sotheby's — the renowned fine art and luxury goods auction house.

As Director of Business Development, her career took her around the world — New York, Paris, London, Beijing, and Hong Kong. Yet she never stopped calling the Bay Area home, returning in 2013 to pursue her true passion in real estate.

Combining deep local knowledge with her business and legal acumen, Victoria has helped buyers, sellers, and investors with all of their real estate needs, specializing in Piedmont, Berkeley, Oakland, and beyond. As a homeowner and seasoned real estate portfolio investor herself, she is not your average realtor.
