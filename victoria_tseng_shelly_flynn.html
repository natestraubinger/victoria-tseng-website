Our Story

Stronger Together

When Victoria Tseng and Shelly Flynn joined forces, it created a rare synergy in East Bay real estate — a partnership built on complementary strengths, shared values, and a relentless commitment to client success.

Victoria brings her global perspective, legal acumen from Cornell Law and Sotheby's, and deep real estate investment expertise. Shelly brings decades of East Bay market mastery, an unparalleled network, and deep community roots through The Grubb Company.

Together, they offer clients something no single agent can: twice the expertise, twice the responsiveness, and twice the negotiating strength — focused on achieving extraordinary outcomes for buyers and sellers across Piedmont, Berkeley, Oakland, and beyond.
